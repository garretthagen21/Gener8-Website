 CONTINUOUS CONNECTED REHABILITATION FOR THE KNEE

Telemedicine was a trend, but now is becoming a permanent part of medicine.  The Gener-8® vision has three phases:

 
 
 Gener-8® device and accessories
will take the patient through all
four phases of rehab per their surgeon’s protocol.

 
 
 The Gener-8® APP transmits essential details specific to the patient’s rehabilitation and progress to their Virtual Medical Professional.  

 
 
 The patient’s Virtual Medical Professional, through a visual interface and real time data will effectively manage the patient, progressing them according to the surgeon’s rehab protocol.

Creating this virtual interface, increases patient’s confidence in their rehab process, overall satisfaction and further ensures positive outcomes. After approximately thirty days, the patient finishes their rehab in an Outpatient Physical Therapy facility.
