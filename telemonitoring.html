Telemonitoring

Telemedicine was a trend — now it is a permanent part of medicine. The Gener-8® vision delivers it in three connected phases.

1

Gener-8® Device

The Gener-8® device and accessories take the patient through all four phases of rehab, per their surgeon’s protocol.

2

The App

The Gener-8® app transmits essential details specific to the patient’s rehabilitation and progress to their Virtual Medical Professional.

3

Virtual Medical Professional

Through a visual interface and real-time data, the Virtual Medical Professional effectively manages the patient, progressing them per the surgeon’s protocol.

Creating this virtual interface increases the patient’s confidence in their rehab process and overall satisfaction, and further ensures positive outcomes. After approximately thirty days, the patient finishes rehab in an outpatient physical-therapy facility.
